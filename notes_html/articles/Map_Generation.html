Map Generation

Description

Galactic Regions

Galactic Regions should are used to give the map some larger scale texture. For example regions could be rich in nebulae, certain minerals, indigenous lifeforms or more or less difficult to traverse. In general every region should have one or multiple themes to generate a more heterogeneous map. This themes might include player spawn themes like "the lonely empire" or "the sector of 3 powers". Balancing this themes could be difficult, but i believe they will be worth it.

Setup Game

To allow a realistic formation for ancient empires a setup game is played within abstract systems (or regions if systems are to small). Different perks allow an earlier or later start. This a extremely simplified version of the game, where interaction is limited to stuff like:

  • Invest in the military
  • Invest in research
  • Colonies a system
  • Invest in a colonized system
  • Wage war
  • Enforce peace
  • Map distant regions
  • up lift primitives
  • strengthen the government
  • Invest in the intelligence service
  • Colonize with sub light ships (leading to offshoot empires instead of directly controlled area)
  • etc.
A turn should be limited to n actions and the mini game should at least track colony development and some sort of fleet capacity to avoid spamming one action and getting ridiculous like an insane navy without any territory.
