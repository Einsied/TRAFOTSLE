Army composition

Armies are the "ground units" or the pseudo-atomic pieces in a ground battle. (It might be better to scrap them and deploy only units nut they might prove a neat abstraction for less deep planetary ground combat). They consist of different units mixed together. They could get a combined arms bonus during deployment to encourage mixing instead of spamming one unit. The idea is that the weakness of the unit count less than its strengths. So mixing anti-Tank infantry and anti-infantry tanks 50:50, results in more damage to tanks than half the added values if the units.

Sea

Considering water should be a factor in ground combat, (to offer the option to do battle on ocean worlds or just the pacific), dive gear for units might be interesting. Of course the should also be ships even if they might be used primarily as garrison units. (This also gives the option of stuff like torpedo bombers and submarines, that do not suffer damage from aircraft)
